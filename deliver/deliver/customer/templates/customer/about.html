ABOUT US

The Online Bakery system is a web application that allows various customers to order food online and get it delivered. This software is platform-independent and flexibly runs on all operating systems and mobile devices. In addition to this, the Bakery ordering system also allows the users to order meals from other local Bakeries and the same will be delivered based on the userâ€™s delivery request.

Bakery owners can provide their bakery information using the web portal. This information will act as the basis for the search results displayed to the user. A receipt is generated which acts as a token for identifying the customer who placed the order through the Online Bakery system.
